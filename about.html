Our Story

More Than a
Dental Clinic

Reface Dentofacial Centre of Excellence was born from a desire to bridge the gap between international dental standards and the warmth of personalised care right here in Chennai. We believe every patient deserves the same quality of treatment that was once only available abroad.

Founded in Kilpauk, our clinic quickly became a trusted destination for patients seeking not just treatments, but complete smile transformations — from simple cleanings to complex jaw surgeries. Our team of internationally trained specialists brings global expertise with a local, compassionate touch.

At Reface, we invest in the latest technology, from 3D virtual jaw planning to Invisalign certification, ensuring that every patient benefits from the most advanced, comfortable and effective care available today.

Our Journey

Milestones That
Shaped Reface

2009

The Beginning

Reface Dentofacial Centre opens its doors in Kilpauk, Chennai with a vision to deliver internationally standard dental care locally.

2013

Invisalign Certification

Reface becomes one of the first certified Invisalign providers in Kilpauk, expanding clear aligner treatment access in Chennai.

2016

Advanced Implantology Unit

Launch of the dedicated Corticobasal and Full-Arch implant program — offering same-day tooth loading for complex cases.

2019

Virtual Jaw Surgery Planning

Introduction of 3D digital jaw surgery simulation — allowing patients to preview their results before orthognathic surgery.

2022

Clinic Expansion

Reface expands with new treatment suites, a dedicated pediatric dental zone, and upgraded digital imaging technology.

Today

5000+ Smiles & Counting

Reface continues to grow as Chennai's most trusted dentofacial centre — with a team of specialists dedicated to your perfect smile.
